Learning

The four-year Bachelor of Science in Engineering program at BUET includes both core Mechanical Engineering courses and foundational science subjects such as Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry. In addition, the curriculum incorporates several courses from the Humanities and Social Sciences to provide a broader academic perspective. See the website of the Department of Mechanical Engineering (ME), BUET.

The course is conducted through 4 consecutive levels, each level is divided into two terms. Details can be obtained from the Undergraduate Booklet.
