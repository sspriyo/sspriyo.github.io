About Me

My name is Symoum Syfullah Priyo (Bn: সাইমুম সাইফুল্লাহ প্রিয়). I come from Chandpur, a district in the south-eastern region of Bangladesh, located on the banks of the mighty river Meghna.

I passed HSC in Science from Notre Dame College, Dhaka in 2025. I'm currently pursuing a BSc in Mechanical Engineering at Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET)

I write articles, upload photographs with educational value, improve content specially in Bangla. I have written and expanded several articles in Bangla Wikipedia since 2020. Access my userpage in Metawiki for details.

I look forward to build my career as a mechanical engineer and serve my community through research or service.
